About Me

🔬

I am a Geoscientist.

My primary research explores how river systems in tectonically active landscapes record and respond to fault dynamics and surface uplift. I focus on fluvial processes such as channel steepness, incision rates, and sediment transport to understand long-term landscape evolution.
